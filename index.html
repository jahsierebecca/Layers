Outer Core
The inner core is incredibly hot. So hot that it is liquid! It is comprised of liquid Nickel and Iron. The liquid movements of the outer core create magnetic fields, which are very important to life on Earth. These fields determine our magnetic poles and also extend to outer space to protect Earth from the Sun's solar wind.
